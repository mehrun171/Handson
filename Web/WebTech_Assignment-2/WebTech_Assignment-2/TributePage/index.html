RATAN NAVAL TATA


This person inspires me every single day. Every time I see him or hear him, my soul gets filled with motivation and devotion toward this GREAT human being. Popularly known as “Man With Golden Heart”, Sir Ratan Tata is a selfless, motivational, kind, and helping personality whom I admire deeply.

Ratan Naval Tata, famously known as “RATAN TATA”, is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist and former chairman of Tata Sons. He served as chairman of the Tata Group from 1990 to 2012, and again from October 2016 through February 2017. He continues to head its charitable trusts. In 2008, he received the Padma Vibhushan, India’s second highest civilian honour, after the Padma Bhushan in 2000.

Achievements and Awards

Ratan Tata received the Padma Bhushan in 2000 and Padma Vibhushan in 2008, awarded by the Government of India. He also received state honours such as the Maharashtra Bhushan in 2006 and Assam Baibhav in 2021 for his contributions to public welfare and healthcare.

His Other Contributions Include:

  • Chosen as successor by JRD Tata in 1991 to lead Tata Sons.
  • Oversaw revenue growth of over 40x and profit growth of over 50x during his tenure.
  • Engineered major acquisitions: Tetley (Tata Tea), Jaguar Land Rover (Tata Motors), Corus (Tata Steel).
  • Invested personally in Snapdeal, Teabox, CashKaro.com, Ola, Xiaomi, Nestaway, and Dogspot.
  • Widely recognized and loved for his philanthropic efforts across India.

To Conclude...

He was among the most successful businessmen in the country and is widely admired for his philanthropic work and visionary leadership.
